Mon Hun Stories 3 Monstie Dex

Welcome to the Monstie Dex!

This is a tool to assist in tracking monstie info in Monster Hunter Stories 3.

Monsties with a light background color and black text are owned.

Monsties with a dark background color and white text are unowned.

Click a Monstie to view its details in the header bar.

The search bar allows for searches where results match any term, separated by ,'s or |'s

If you instead separate terms by +'s or &'s, every term must match for results to show

A search term of just numbers will show you Monsties whose rank matches any of those numbers, otherwise the term will be tested against the Monstie's name, element, type, or genus. Location and skills to be added

The spoilers toggle will hide information about Monsties not marked as seen or owned. Warning: The header bar will currently show that information anyway if you click them.

Currently you cannot edit your save file, the data you are seeing is just what I have hard coded into the application. Details I haven't found in game are also not yet available. Working on it tho
